Dr. David Blitzer
D.C., R.M.T., Honours B.A.

Dr. David Blitzer is a Chiropractor and Registered Massage Therapist, born and raised in Thornhill, Ontario. From a young age, David was always highly involved in sports and exercise. He loves running, swimming and biking, hockey, tennis, golf, water and snow skiing, weight lifting and yoga. Over the years, David’s commitment to athletics has won him many awards ranging from Most Proficient to Most Dedicated in his field.

His strong passion for Chiropractic, Human Biomechanics and Functional Movement started when he began to experience sever headaches and neck pain due to postural strain from long hours of studying with poor ergonomic considerations. Chiropractic helped David overcome this life obstacle and return to wellness.

David is highly qualified, with several degrees including a Bachelor’s degree from Wilfrid Laurier’s Department of Science in Psychology, a Diploma in Registered Massage Therapy, and a Doctor of Chiropractic from Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College.

He holds international designations as a Doctor of Chiropractic in Canada and the United States, is TPI certified, and is constantly taking courses to expand his knowledge base. He has performed many community outreaches, from onsite event physical therapy to concussion screenings and aqua therapy. He has worked with a wide range of individuals including high level athletes, the elderly, children and the disabled.

Dr. Blitzer loves what he does, and goes to work every day with a smile on his face.

What Is Modern Chiropractic?

More than just an adjustment, modern chiropractic is a form of alternative medicine specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of mechanical disorders of the musculoskeletal and nervous system. Diagnosis is achieved through interpreting the patient’s report of their physical ailment and using orthopedic physical examination testing. Treatment involves a wide range of approaches depending on the condition, and is predominantly based on manual therapy and physical rehabilitation. Ultimately, the goal of Chiropractic is to guide the patient out of pain to become healthy and physically stronger.

Your First Visit




Your fist visit will last 45-60 minutes and comprehensively investigates your chief complaint. The visit includes a global evaluation of your health, via a history, physical examination and analysis of daily activities. A report of findings is discussed, where the patient is explained what is causing the problem and how we intend to fix it. For each patient, a unique plan of management will be constructed to optimize therapeutic success. Appropriate therapy and treatment will follow as needed.

Our Unique Method

At the Medical Centre, we take a unique approach to health through the evaluation of each case with the appropriate member of our team of health professionals. The team is composed of Chiropractors, Medical Doctors, a Chronic Pain Specialist, and Massage Therapists. Thus, every avenue of treatment and health is considered. We understand that each case is unique, and therapy must be progressive, to resolve issues and alleviate pain.
